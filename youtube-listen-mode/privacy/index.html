YouTube Listen Mode Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 2025

Introduction

Thank you for using YouTube Listen Mode. This Privacy Policy explains that we do not collect, store, or share your personal information or usage data.

Data Collection And Use

YouTube Listen Mode does not collect user data.

No personal information: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any other personally identifiable information.

No tracking: We do not track your browsing history, search queries, or website usage.

No transmission: All operations performed by the extension happen locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers or third parties.

Permissions

To function correctly, YouTube Listen Mode may require specific browser permissions, such as access to the active tab or specific websites. These permissions are used only to perform the extension's core functionality and are not used to collect data.

Third-Party Services

YouTube Listen Mode may be hosted on browser extension stores such as the Chrome Web Store, Firefox Add-ons, or Microsoft Edge Add-ons. While the extension does not collect data, those platform providers may collect technical usage data governed by their own privacy policies.
