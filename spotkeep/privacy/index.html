SpotKeep Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-01

SpotKeep is a personal iPhone app for saving where you parked or where you stored things.

Data Collection

SpotKeep does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal data.

The spots, notes, categories, parking details, and photos you add are stored locally on your device. They are used only to show your saved spots inside the app.

Photos And Camera

SpotKeep may ask for access to your camera or photo library when you choose to attach a reference photo to a saved spot.

Photo access is optional. Photos selected or captured in SpotKeep are used only for the saved spot you attach them to.

Accounts

SpotKeep does not require an account.

Analytics And Tracking

SpotKeep does not use advertising, third-party tracking, or analytics services in the current release.

Children

SpotKeep is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

If SpotKeep changes how data is handled, this policy will be updated before the change is released.
