Common Questions

Do I Need An Account?

No. SpotKeep does not require an account.

Where Is My Data Stored?

Your saved spots are stored locally on your iPhone.

Why Does SpotKeep Ask For Camera Or Photo Access?

Only when you choose to attach a reference photo to a saved parking spot or saved thing.

Can I Use SpotKeep Without Photos?

Yes. Photos are optional.

How Do I Delete A Saved Spot?

Open the saved spot and use the delete or archive action.

Does SpotKeep Track My Location?

No. The current version does not track your live location. You type the place details you want to remember.
